Blog About

I'm currently studying the dynamics of neural networks by studying/proposing neuromorphic architectures. This blog is the culmination of my work following the maxim "if it's makes sense in silicon, it makes sense in software".

My background is in Mathematics with a BSc from the Open University and two years of MSc Mathematics @ Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I then did a few years as a paperweight in the cybersecurity industry before teaching myself how to make chips online, going back to get a second MSc in Embedded AI @ Maynooth University and undertaking this PhD.

If you feel the need to reach out - you can find me on LinkedIn and my email's out there somewhere!
